About Us


BALAJI Food Prodcuts carries a legacy of 4+ decades in agricultural food ingredient processing and packaging. Family-owned and operated, BALAJI Food Prodcuts is dedicated to providing healthy, functional foods to the marketplace and is vertically integrated to maximize control. With products shipping regularly to over a dozen countries throughout the world BALAJI Food Prodcuts has the expertise required to maximize competitiveness and helped by having 4 facility locations in and around Tamilnadu, is vertically integrated to maximize control.
